The Untold Adventure

2022

In collaboration with MUHBA (Museo de Historia de Barcelona)

Elle

2021

In collaboration with IDEAL (Centro de Artes Digitales de Barcelona) & VIBE

There is undoubtedly an increasing trend toward the use of the Elle in some Spanish-speaking countries, such as Argentina, Spain or Mexico. That is because of the need to use a non-gender article that helps to include the entire population.

This project considers the possibility of a future where gender differentiation would not exist. Within this future, we have focused on the idea of language, since it is a system that we use on a daily basis, and that helps to create our identity and show who we are to the world.

Reflection

2020


The walls of the confessional as an element of privacy, to isolate the user from the outside. Today, and referring to the confessional as a modern element in which its meaning expands to contract new spaces, digital actuality, in which physical walls can be transformed into virtual, where they are not necessary because only the person in front of the screen understands the meaning of their confession.

Empowering people to say how they feel, this confessional will ask different questions according to their emotions.
